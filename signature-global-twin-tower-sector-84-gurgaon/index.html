Signature Twin Tower - New Projects in Sector 84 Gurgaon

Signature DeLuxe DXP and Signature Titanium SPR were huge successes, and now Signature Global is thrilled to announce the opening of Twin Towers in Sector 84, New Gurgaon. In the soon-to-be Global City, Signature Global Twin Towers DXP Sector 84 Gurgaon is ideally located close to the Dwarka Expressway. The project’s most notable feature is its ideal location, which puts it in close proximity to important social infrastructure like colleges, hospitals, schools, and shopping centers.

Rising prominently in the heart of Gurugram, Signature Twin Tower offers a modern blend of design, convenience, and community living. Strategically located in Sector 84, these twin towers bring a seamless connection to the city’s key business hubs while maintaining a serene residential environment.Designed for families seeking space and comfort, the project features 3 & 4.5 BHK flats, thoughtfully planned to maximize natural light and ventilation. Each apartment reflects a contemporary architectural vision, combining functionality with elegance.Residents at Twin Tower DXP enjoy access to a wide range of amenities, from landscaped gardens and jogging tracks to well-equipped fitness centers and recreational spaces. Safety and security are integral to the design, ensuring peace of mind alongside lifestyle comforts.

Beyond the towers, Sector 84 offers excellent connectivity to educational institutions, healthcare facilities, shopping destinations, and entertainment hubs, making daily life convenient and efficient. The area is fast emerging as one of Gurugram’s most sought-after residential neighborhoods, balancing urban accessibility with tranquil living.With its strategic location, spacious 3 & 4.5 BHK flats, and thoughtfully curated amenities, Signature Twin Tower stands as a contemporary landmark for modern living in Gurugram. Whether for families or professionals seeking a well-connected yet peaceful home, this twin tower project represents a harmonious blend of lifestyle, convenience, and architectural finesse.

About Signature Global

Signature Global is the Largest real estate development company in Delhi-NCR, focussed on affordable and mid segment housing (LIG and MIG) in terms of unit supplied (in the below Rs.80 lakh price category) between 2019 and 2021 with a market share of 19%.

The company commenced its operations in 2014 through its subsidiary, Signature Global Private Limited, with the launch of our Solera project on 6.13 acres of land in Gurugram, Haryana. We have grown our operations over the years and in less than a decade, and as of March 31, 2022, we had sold 23,453 residential and commercial units, all within the Delhi NCR region, with an aggregate Saleable Area of 14.59 million square feet.
